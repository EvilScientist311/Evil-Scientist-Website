Mechanics

The study of motion, forces, and energy in physical systems. Encompasses classical mechanics, fluid dynamics, and the principles of conservation.

Related Papers

Lagrangian Mechanics

Lagrangian mechanics is a reformulation of classical mechanics in terms of energy. It provides an elegant way to derive equations of motion.

Celestial Mechanics

The study of the motion of celestial bodies under the influence of gravitational forces. It includes the derivation and analysis of Kepler's laws.

The Legendre Transform

The Legendre transform is a mathematical operation that transforms between the Lagrangian and Hamiltonian formulations of mechanics.
